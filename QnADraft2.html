Interview Assistant for a Petition for Appointment of a Guardian for an Incapacitated Person

Please Note: This interview just helps you prepare the petition. It does not give you any legal rights. You will need to file these documents with the court and a judge will make the decisions about your case.

This interview will help you fill out a "Petition for Appointment of Guardian for an Incapacitated Person" and assist you in beginning the process of appointing a guardianship for an incapacitated loved one. See a sample of the petition here.

Answering these questions should take approximately 30-45 minutes to complete. Once you have completed this interview, you will be prompted to download two documents: 1) a partially completed Adult Guardianship Petition, and 2) a summary of what your next steps will be.

Also note that from here on out we will refer to the incapacitated individual as "Respondent," as that is how they are referred to in all court documents.
